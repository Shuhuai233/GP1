## Status Effects (the foundation everything builds on)

| Status | How applied | Effect | Visual |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Poison** | Venom Round hits | Stacks. No decay. Consumed by Detonator for burst. | Green glow, stack number |
| **Burn** | Incendiary Round hits, Flashfire spell | +20% damage from ALL sources. 5 sec, refreshes. | Orange flames |
| **Slow** | Frost Round hits | -50% move speed. 4 sec, refreshes. | Cyan frost particles |
| **Shock** | Volt Round hits | Chains to 1 nearby enemy within 5m. 3 sec. | Blue-white sparks |
| **Mark** | Spotter spell | +30% damage from ALL sources. 6 sec. Doesn't refresh — one application. | Red crosshair icon on enemy |

## Status Combos (the 1+1>2 payoffs)

When TWO statuses are active on the same enemy simultaneously:

| Combo | Name | Effect | Why it's exciting |
|---|---|---|---|
| Poison + Burn | **Toxic Fire** | Poison stacks deal 2x damage when consumed by Detonator | Burn supercharges the execute |
| Poison + Shock | **Contagion** | When Detonator consumes poison, 50% of stacks spread to shocked-chain targets | AoE execute |
| Burn + Slow | **Meltdown** | Burn damage ticks twice as fast on slowed enemies | CC enables damage |
| Shock + Mark | **Overload** | Marked+shocked enemy explodes on death, 20 dmg AoE (5m) | Chain reaction |
| Slow + Poison | **Festering** | Slowed enemies gain +1 bonus poison stack per Venom hit (total +3 instead of +2) | CC accelerates stacking |

## SYNERGY CHAINS (the "build paths" players discover)

### Build 1: Poison Execute
**Cards:** Venom Round → (stack poison) → Detonator (consume stacks)
**Enhancers:** Scatter Round (3x stacking speed), Catalyst Round (+1 to all stacks/hit), Toxin Bomb (instant 5 stacks AoE)
**Combo:** Add Incendiary → Toxic Fire → Detonator does 6x instead of 3x
**Peak moment:** 20 poison stacks + Burn + Detonator = 120 bonus damage in one click

### Build 2: Shock Chain
**Cards:** Volt Round → (apply shock, chains to nearby) → Chain Lightning (bounces to 3 more)
**Enhancers:** Spotter (Mark target first → Overload on death = AoE explosion), Infusion (2x shock chains)
**Combo:** Volt + Spotter → Shock + Mark = Overload → enemy explodes on death
**Peak moment:** Room full of shocked+marked enemies, Chain Lightning triggers, explosions cascade

### Build 3: Slow Burn
**Cards:** Frost Round → (slow enemies) → Incendiary Round → (burn ticks 2x on slowed = Meltdown)
**Enhancers:** Flashfire (burn everything), Overclock (more bullets = more burn application)
**Combo:** Frost + Incendiary = Meltdown (burn ticks 2x). Add Venom = Festering (+3 stacks/hit instead of +2)
**Peak moment:** Everything is slow, everything is burning, burn ticks are doubled, poison stacking faster than normal

### Build 4: Multi-Status
**Cards:** Apply 3+ statuses → Purge (consume ALL for 10 dmg per type)
**Enhancers:** Resonance Round (+25% dmg on multi-status enemies), Infusion (2x status strength)
**Combo:** Venom + Incendiary + Frost + Volt on one target = 4 statuses → Purge = 40 bonus dmg
**Peak moment:** "How many statuses can I stack before Purging?" — the collector's build

### Build 5: Sustain Tank
**Cards:** Drain Round (heal on hit) + Barrier (shield) + Vampiric Burst (heal burst)
**Enhancers:** Phase Step (dodge), Reload Surge (instant reshuffle to get more defensive cards)
**Combo:** Drain + Vampiric Burst = heal from both sources simultaneously = nearly unkillable for 3 sec
**Peak moment:** Wave 3 Rusher swarm, pop Barrier + Vampiric Burst, Drain Round firing, health going UP while being swarmed

## CARD ACQUISITION PACING

### Starter deck (9 cards):
- 3x Standard Round (F)
- 1x Venom Round (F)
- 1x Incendiary Round (F)
- 1x Piercing Round (F)
- 1x Detonator (S)
- 1x Barrier (S)
- 1x Flashfire (S)

### Between waves — pick 1 from 3 (mixed pool):
- Wave 1 clear → pick 1 → deck = 10
- Wave 2 clear → pick 1 → deck = 11
- (Future: more waves = more picks = deeper builds)

### Card offering rules:
- Never offer Standard Round (always available as filler)
- Offer mix of firing and function cards
- Weight toward cards that synergize with what player already has (soft guidance, not forced)
- Show card type icon (gun or spell) so player knows which hand it goes to
