# GP1 — One Pager

## The Pitch
**FPS roguelite with gun customization and spell casting.** Collect weapons, attach mods that transform how they fire, cast spells to load special ammo or protect yourself. Build your loadout. Survive the waves.

## The Hook (5 seconds)
Find guns. Mod them. Cast spell ammo. Shoot everything.

## Core Fantasy
You're fighting through endless waves of enemies. You carry two guns — each one customized with attachments you've found. Split Barrel makes your AR fire 3 bullets per shot. Explosive Tips make your Revolver bullets detonate on impact. Between fights, you cast spells: load Poison Magazine into your SMG to stack venom on everything, then trigger Detonator to blow up all those stacks in one massive hit. Your guns get crazier. Your spells get stronger. Every run builds differently.

## Genre
First-person shooter + roguelite + gun customization + spell casting

## Unique Selling Points
1. **Weapons with attachments** — find guns, mod them with game-changing attachments (split bullets, explosions, ricochet, chain lightning). Not +15% stat bumps — dramatic, visible transformations.
2. **Magazine spells** — cast Poison/Fire/Shock/Frost/Explosive ammo onto your current magazine. Turns any gun into a status-applying machine. Reload clears it — timing your cast matters.
3. **Stack-then-Execute (叠层后斩杀)** — stack status effects with your modded gun + magazine spell, then cast a finisher spell for massive burst damage.
4. **Attachment × Magazine interactions** — Split Barrel + Poison Magazine = 3 poison bullets per shot. Every combo is a discovery.

## Core Loop
1. **Shoot** — fire your modded weapons at enemies
2. **Cast** — load magazine spells for special ammo, or use character spells (shield, dash, blink)
3. **Execute** — trigger finisher spells to detonate status stacks for burst damage
4. **Survive** — dodge visible enemy projectiles, use cover, cast defensive spells
5. **Choose** — between waves, pick new weapons, attachments, or function cards
6. **Build** — your 2 guns get more attachments, your spell hand gets more options, combos emerge

## Target Audience
Players who love: gun modding (Escape from Tarkov's weapon building) + spell combos (Slay the Spire's synergy discovery) + fast FPS combat (DOOM Eternal's pace)
