# GP1 — One Pager

## The Pitch
**FPS roguelite deckbuilder.** Your deck is your magazine. Each card is a pack of bullets with an effect. Your left hand casts spells. Build a powerful deck. Every run is different.

## The Hook (5 seconds)
Your gun fires cards. Your spells are cards. Build the deck. Shoot the deck.

## Core Fantasy
You're a scavenger fighting through waves of enemies. Your weapon fires card-packs — bursts of poison bullets, fire bullets, piercing rounds. Your off-hand holds spell cards — detonate poison stacks, cast shields, dash through danger. Between waves you pick new cards, building toward devastating combos. The deeper you go, the stronger your build — but one mistake and you're dead.

## Genre
First-person shooter + roguelite + deckbuilder

## Unique Selling Points
1. **Cards ARE your ammo** — not passive modifiers, each card pack changes what your gun does
2. **Two-hand system** — right hand fires (reactive), left hand casts spells (proactive decisions)
3. **Status combos** — Poison + Burn = Toxic Fire. Two simple cards create a third emergent effect.
4. **Stack-then-Execute (叠层后斩杀)** — build up status stacks, then trigger a finisher spell for massive burst

## Core Loop
1. **Fight** — shoot card-packs from your gun, cast spells from your off-hand
2. **Survive** — use cover, dash, shield, drain health from kills
3. **Choose** — pick 1 new card from 3 between waves (firing card or spell)
4. **Build** — your deck grows, synergies emerge, your build comes online
5. **Repeat** — each wave harder, each reload a fresh shuffle

## Target Audience
Players who love: Slay the Spire's deckbuilding + DOOM's shooting + Vampire Survivors' build-comes-online dopamine

## Platform
PC (Steam). Godot 4.x. Part-time solo dev.

## Comparable Games
| Game | What GP1 borrows | What GP1 does differently |
|---|---|---|
| Slay the Spire | Deckbuilding, synergy discovery, pick-1-from-3 | Real-time FPS combat, not turn-based |
| DOOM Eternal | Fast shooting, two-hand abilities | Cards as ammo, deckbuilding progression |
| Vampire Crawlers | First-person, cards, roguelite | Real-time aiming (not turn-based), deeper synergies |
| Gunfire Reborn | FPS roguelite | Cards are active bullet effects, not passive scrolls |
