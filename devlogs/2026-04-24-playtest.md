# Playtest Feedback: 2026-04-24

## Context
First playtest of Gate 1 prototype. Player tested the full 3-wave loop.

---

## Feedback Summary

### 1. Card visibility — WORKS, needs foresight
- **Positive:** Player can notice which card they're currently using via color language
- **Request:** Want to see upcoming cards in the magazine (foresight)
- **Decision:** Show next 2 upcoming cards as colored indicators

### 2. 3C — Everything feels off
Multiple issues reported simultaneously:
- Movement feels floaty / slippery
- Movement feels sluggish / heavy (contradictory with floaty — likely different axes or states)
- Mouse look feels off
- Firing feels weak (no punch / impact)
- Jump feels wrong
- ADS transition is clunky
- Spatial awareness is poor
- General "nothing feels tight"

**This is the highest priority fix.** If 3C doesn't feel good, nothing else matters. The card system, enemies, UI — all irrelevant if the base FPS feel is bad.

### 3. Player HP too low
- Dies too quickly. Current 100 HP with enemy damage values feels fragile.
- Options: raise HP to 150-200, lower enemy damage, add health pickups, or add passive regen

### 4. Cards not diverse enough
- Only 5 card types. Expected feedback. Need to design more.
- Priority after 3C is fixed.

### 5. Enemy status/HP not visible enough
- Poison glow and stack numbers exist but too subtle
- Need: clearer enemy health bars, bigger status indicators

### 6. Enemy variety
- Player only noticed 1 enemy type despite 3 existing (Grunt, Big Eye, Rusher)
- Placeholder meshes (colored capsules) are not visually distinct enough
- All enemies read as "generic humanoid shape" regardless of type
- Need: dramatically different silhouettes, sizes, colors, movement patterns

---

## Priority Actions

### P0 — Fix 3C (blocks everything)
The entire 3C needs a tuning pass. Current values from 3C_DOCUMENT.md may not match what was built, or the values themselves need adjustment.

**Specific investigations needed:**
1. **Floaty + sluggish simultaneously:** Likely acceleration is too low (sluggish to start) and deceleration is too low (floaty to stop). Check ground_accel and ground_decel values. May need higher values or different curve.
2. **Mouse look off:** Check if raw mouse input is being used correctly. Any frame-rate dependent issues? Delta applied incorrectly? Sensitivity too high/low?
3. **Firing feels weak:** No audio, no weapon model, minimal screen feedback. Fire punch needs: stronger camera kick, more muzzle flash, screen shake (re-evaluate the "no screen shake" rule), hit sound, weapon animation.
4. **Jump wrong:** Check gravity (20 m/s²), jump velocity (5 m/s), air control (30%). Might need tuning.
5. **ADS clunky:** Check FOV interpolation speed (spec says 0.15s). May need faster or different easing.
6. **Spatial awareness poor:** No minimap, arena is graybox with same-colored everything. Need floor markings, distinct cover colors, better lighting.

### P1 — Enemy visual distinction
- Big Eye should look NOTHING like a Grunt
- Rusher should be visually aggressive (different color, size, posture, movement speed visible at a glance)
- Add floating health bars above enemies
- Make status effects (poison glow, burn particles) much larger / more obvious

### P2 — Player survivability
- Increase HP from 100 to 150
- Or reduce Grunt damage from 5 to 3 per shot
- Or add health pickups on enemy kill (small heal, 5-10 HP)
- Test which feels best

### P3 — Card foresight UI
- Show next 2 cards as colored dots/indicators near crosshair or on weapon
- Player can plan 1-2 shots ahead

### P4 — More card types
- Design 5-10 additional cards after 3C is fixed
- Focus on cards that feel DIFFERENT from each other (not just stat variants)

### P5 — More enemy types
- Design 2-3 additional enemy types after visual distinction is fixed
- Each must have dramatically different silhouette and behavior
