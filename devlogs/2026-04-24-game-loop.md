# Game Loop Document: 2026-04-24

## Purpose

Map out every step of the player experience from launch to end. Identify mismatches between design intent and current implementation.

---

## Current Loop (what's actually built)

### Step 1: Game Start
- Scene loads. Player placed at PlayerSpawn (0, 1, 15).
- 1 second delay.
- Wave 1 begins automatically.
- Player has: 150 HP (after fix), Revolver, Starter deck (3x Standard + 1x Venom + 1x Incendiary + 1x Piercing).
- Deck shuffles, top 6 cards load into magazine.

### Step 2: Combat (Wave)
- Enemies spawn at EnemySpawn points.
- Player shoots. Each trigger pull fires 1 bullet from the current card pack.
- After ~6 bullets (3 for Detonator), current card pack is exhausted. Next card pack in magazine activates. Color shifts.
- Player sees: crosshair color change, muzzle flash color, bullet trail color, hit effect color.
- On hit: enemy takes damage (with burn multiplier if burning). Status effects applied (poison stacks, burn timer).
- On kill: enemy dies (shrink + free). Kill healing +10 HP (after fix).
- When magazine empties (6 card packs fired): player must reload (R key or auto-reload on empty fire).
- Reload takes 2 seconds. Full deck reshuffles, top 6 load into new magazine.
- Mid-pack reload: remaining bullets wasted. Strategic choice.
- Player can move, sprint, jump, crouch, ADS throughout.
- Enemies attack: Grunt shoots (3 dmg/sec), Big Eye charges beam (20 dmg), Rusher rushes melee (10 dmg).
- Player takes damage: red flash, camera flinch, HP decreases.

### Step 3: Wave Clear
- All enemies in wave dead.
- "WAVE X CLEARED" text appears (1.5 sec).
- Card selection UI appears immediately (no pause timer currently).

### Step 4: Card Selection (between waves)
- Game pauses (Engine.time_scale = 0? or process paused?).
- Mouse cursor appears.
- 3 random cards shown from pool of all 5 types.
- Player clicks one. Card added to deck (deck grows: 7 after wave 1, 8 after wave 2).
- UI closes. 1 second delay. Next wave starts.
- NO healing between waves currently.

### Step 5: Repeat Steps 2-4
- Wave 2: 2 Grunts + 1 Big Eye.
- Wave 3: 1 Big Eye + 4 Rushers + 2 Grunts.

### Step 6: Run End
- **Win:** All 3 waves cleared. "ARENA CLEARED!" text. Restart button. Mouse visible.
- **Lose:** Player HP reaches 0. Death camera (drop, tilt, roll). "YOU DIED" text. Restart button. Mouse visible.
- Restart reloads entire scene. No persistence. No extraction. No meta-progression.

## Identified Mismatches & Gaps

### Mismatch 1: No healing anywhere
- **Design says:** Kill healing 10 HP (fix plan), between-wave heal (discussed)
- **Built:** Zero healing of any kind
- **Impact:** Player bleeds out across waves with no recovery. Wave 3 is nearly impossible at low HP.
- **Fix:** Implement kill healing + 25 HP heal between waves.

### Mismatch 2: No extraction mechanic
- **Design says:** "Extract to keep your build. Die and lose everything."
- **Built:** Win = restart. Lose = restart. No extraction option.
- **Impact:** The second hook ("extract to keep your deck") doesn't exist. No risk/reward decision.
- **Status:** Expected for Gate 1. Extraction is a v0.5 feature. But the loop should ACKNOWLEDGE it exists conceptually — maybe a "portal spawns after wave 2, extract early for partial reward or push to wave 3."

### Mismatch 3: No between-wave pause
- **Design says:** "10-15 sec pause, pick 1 from 3 cards"
- **Built:** Card selection appears immediately after wave clear text. No breathing room.
- **Impact:** Player goes from combat adrenaline straight to menu. No decompression. No time to check HP, evaluate deck, plan next wave.
- **Fix:** Add 3-5 second pause after "WAVE CLEARED" before card selection appears. Show current deck summary during this pause.

### Mismatch 4: Card selection pool too small
- **Design says:** Pick from offered cards (implies variety)
- **Built:** Offers 3 random from the same 5 card types. Player might see Standard Round offered — why would they pick it? Also might see duplicates of cards they already have.
- **Impact:** Choices feel meaningless. "Pick the best of 3" when you've already seen all 5 types.
- **Fix for now:** Weight offerings away from Standard Round (never offer it). With 5 new cards in the pipeline (Frost, Drain, Scatter, Ricochet, Overcharge), this problem solves itself through content volume.

### Mismatch 5: No deck inspection
- **Design says:** "Between rooms: full deck inspection, card details, tooltips"
- **Built:** No way to see your current deck composition.
- **Impact:** Player can't strategize. Don't know how many Venom cards they have. Don't know if Detonator is in their deck.
- **Fix:** Add a deck summary panel visible during card selection (show all cards in deck as colored icons with counts).

### Mismatch 6: No card foresight during combat
- **Design says (from playtest feedback):** Player wants to see next 2 cards
- **Built:** Only current card visible via color.
- **Impact:** Player can't plan ahead. Discussed in fix plan as P3.

### Mismatch 7: No grace period after card selection
- **Design says (3C doc):** "Brief 0.5s grace period (no enemy damage) to re-orient"
- **Built:** Combat resumes immediately after card pick.
- **Impact:** Player picks a card, enemies are already shooting. Disorienting.
- **Fix:** 0.5s invulnerability + no enemy firing after card selection closes.

### Mismatch 8: Restart is full scene reload
- **Design says:** Quick restart, Hotline Miami feel.
- **Built:** get_tree().reload_current_scene() — reloads everything from scratch.
- **Impact:** May feel slow depending on load time. No run stats, no "what happened" summary.
- **Fix for later:** Add a brief death summary ("You survived 2 waves, fired 36 cards, dealt 480 damage, killed 6 enemies"). Motivates replay.

## The Loop As It SHOULD Be (design target)

### Moment-to-moment (during combat, ~3 second cycle):
1. See crosshair color (identify current card mode)
2. Choose target based on card type (Venom -> Big Eye, Incendiary -> groups)
3. Fire 6 bullets (~3 sec)
4. Card pack transitions — color shifts
5. React to new card, adjust target
6. Repeat until magazine empty

### Tactical (during magazine, ~18 sec cycle):
1. Magazine loads 6 card packs (random order from deck)
2. Fire through all 6 packs
3. DECISION: reload now (waste remaining bullets for fresh shuffle) or keep firing current pack?
4. Reload (2 sec vulnerability)
5. New shuffle, new 6 packs loaded
6. Repeat

### Strategic (during wave, ~60-90 sec):
1. Wave starts. Enemies spawn.
2. Evaluate threats (Big Eye at distance, Rushers closing, Grunts flanking)
3. Prioritize targets based on current card and upcoming cards
4. Manage HP (use cover, kill for healing)
5. Clear all enemies

### Between waves (~15 sec):
1. Wave clear announcement (3 sec)
2. Breathing room — see current HP, deck state (5 sec)
3. Card selection (pick 1 from 3) — evaluate based on what you already have
4. Heal 25 HP
5. Next wave countdown (3 sec)
6. Grace period (0.5 sec invulnerability)

### Meta (across full run, ~5-10 min):
1. Start with starter deck (3 Standard + 1 Venom + 1 Incendiary + 1 Piercing)
2. Wave 1: learn the system
3. Pick a card -> deck grows to 7
4. Wave 2: experience the Big Eye (stack target). Test new card.
5. Pick a card -> deck grows to 8
6. Wave 3: pressure test. All enemy types.
7. Win or die. Restart.

### Future meta (v0.5+):
- Extraction portal appears after wave 2
- Extract = keep cards for meta-progression (unlock pool), end run early
- Push to wave 3 = more rewards but risk losing everything
- After run: cards extracted unlock in the card pool for future runs
- Next run: starter deck draws from expanded pool

## What to tell the tech session

The current loop is MISSING these between-combat elements:

1. **Kill healing** (10 HP per kill) — in fix plan
2. **Between-wave heal** (25 HP flat) — add to fix plan
3. **Between-wave pause** (3-5 sec breathing room before card selection)
4. **Deck inspection panel** (during card selection, show current deck)
5. **Card foresight** (next 2 cards as colored pips during combat)
6. **Grace period** (0.5s invulnerability after card selection)
7. **Never offer Standard Round** in card selection (weight it out)

Items 1, 2, 3, 6, 7 are quick implementation. Items 4, 5 are moderate UI work.
